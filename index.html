Technique guide

Brushing — adults

1
Use a soft-bristled brush and a pea-sized amount of fluoride toothpaste.
2
Hold the brush at a 45° angle to your gums.
3
Use short, gentle circular strokes — never harsh scrubbing.
4
Brush outer, inner, and chewing surfaces for 2 full minutes.
Pro tip

Use the built-in 2-minute timer on the home screen.

Flossing — adults

1
Use 18 inches of floss, sliding gently between teeth in a zigzag.
2
Curve into a C-shape around each tooth and slide beneath the gumline.

Brushing — children (ages 3–12)

1
Under 3: rice-grain of toothpaste. Ages 3–6: pea-sized amount.
2
Help children brush until they can tie their own shoelaces (age ~7–8).
3
Spit but don't rinse — keeps fluoride protection on teeth.
Parent tip

Brush alongside your child — kids learn by watching.

Brushing with braces

1
Brush at 45° above and below brackets to clean all surfaces.
2
Brush after every meal — aim for 3 times daily minimum.
Must-have

Interdental brushes slide under the wire to clean between brackets.

Cleaning full dentures

1
Use a soft denture brush and non-abrasive paste — never regular toothpaste.
2
Soak overnight in denture solution or plain water.
3
Brush gums, tongue, and palate each morning before reinserting.
Important

Never sleep with dentures in — rest time prevents irritation.
